'''Andrew Forson''' is a fictional character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]].

==Publication history==
{{expand-section|date=March 2016}}
Andrew Forson first appeared in ''[[Fantastic Four]]'' #610 and was created by [[Jonathan Hickman]] and [[Ryan Stegman]]

==Fictional character biography==
Andrew Forson was the [[Scientist Supreme]] of [[Advanced Idea Mechanics|A.I.M.]] after the organization became public and established their base in the now sovereign island Barbuda and overthrew the [[Wizard (Marvel Comics)|Wizard]] with consent of the rest of A.I.M. Barbuba was then renamed A.I.M. Island<ref>''Fantastic Four'' #610 (November 2012)</ref>

Forson appears as the Supreme Leader of the new High Council of A.I.M. consisting of [[Graviton (comics)|Graviton]] (who works as the Minister of Science), [[Jude the Entropic Man]] (who works as the [[Health minister|Minister of Health]]), [[Mentallo]] (who works as the Minister of Home Affairs), [[Superia]] (who works as the [[Education minister|Minister of Education]] in Bagalia), [[Black Widow (Yelena Belova)|Yelena Belova]] (who works as the [[Minister of State]] in Bagalia), and an undercover [[Taskmaster (comics)|Taskmaster]] (who works as the [[Defence minister|Minister of Defense]]).<ref>''Secret Avengers'' Vol. 2 #2</ref> Forson then leads A.I.M. into attending a weapons expo which led to A.I.M. fighting against the [[Secret Avengers]]. During the battle, Forson takes the opportunity to steal the [[Iron Patriot]] armor.<ref>''Secret Avengers'' Vol. 2 #3</ref> [[Daisy Johnson]] launched an unsanctioned operation to send the Secret Avengers to A.I.M. Island to assassinate Forson, and the group seemingly killed him. Despite Johnson ended up suspended for breaking protocol and [[Maria Hill]] is put in charge of S.H.I.E.L.D. again, Forson was revealed to be alive all along, the news of A.I.M. being a new permanent member of the Security Council is known.<ref>''Secret Avengers'' Vol. 2 #5</ref>

Forson finds Superia when she is badly injured after an incident on A.I.M. Island regarding an escaped creature.<ref>''Avengers'' Vol. 5 #15</ref> An A.I.M. Agent then approaches Forson and Superia showing them a 2-D hologram of the Organism beating the Avengers. Taking pride in this, Forson orders their translocator activated and to retrieve their "lost child".<ref>''Avengers'' Vol. 5 #16</ref>

When Taskmaster gets the chance to get [[Mockingbird (Marvel Comics)|Mockingbird]] off the island, she doesn't respond to anything he says until both are captured. While being interrogated, Taskmaster is shot and seemingly killed by Mockingbird who is now under the control of Forson.<ref>''Secret Avengers'' Vol. 2 #13</ref> Forson revealed that Mockingbird was secretly working for Forson years ago, and that he used Mockingbird to "get [[Victorius (comics)|Victorious]] out of the way" so that he could take over the Cult of Entropy.<ref>''Secret Avengers'' Vol. 2 #14 (March 2014)</ref>

Using an as-yet-unidentified device in the pages of ''[[Avengers World]]'', Forson and A.I.M. accelerate the flow of time in the limits of A.I.M. Island creating in a matter of hours for the real world year of progress and transforming A.I.M. into a technologically advanced empire.<ref>''Avengers World'' #1</ref> When [[Smasher]] comes to the island, Forson has the Entropic Man transform her into his messenger.<ref>''Avengers World'' #2</ref>

==Powers and abilities==
Andrew Forson is an experienced scientist with genius-level intellect.
