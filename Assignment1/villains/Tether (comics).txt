'''Tether''' is a fictional [[Mutant (Marvel Comics)|mutant]] character appearing in [[American comic book]]s published by [[Marvel Comics]]. Her first appearance was in ''Uncanny X-Men '97 Annual''.

==Fictional character biography==
Tether was a member of [[Gene Nation]], the group of second generation [[Morlocks (comics)|Morlocks]] whom [[Storm (Marvel Comics)|Storm]] encountered and relocated to [[Africa]].{{issue|date=December 2012}}

When [[Humanity's Last Stand]] attacked the Gene Nationals, Storm and the [[X-Men]] arrived to help. Unfortunately, so did [[Havok (comics)|Havok]]'s [[Brotherhood of Evil Mutants|Brotherhood of Mutants]] who offered the Gene Nationals protection against Humanity's Last Stand. Humanity's Last Stand were eventually defeated, and Tether (along with fellow Gene National [[Boost (comics)|Boost]]) sought to leave, helping the Brotherhood escape in return for being dropped off at an unrevealed place of their choosing.{{issue|date=December 2012}}

==Powers and abilities==
Tether is a mutant with a reptilian appearance. She possesses razor-sharp claws and fangs and an electrified tail.
